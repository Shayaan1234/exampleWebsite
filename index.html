About Mario

Mario is a fictional character in the Mario video game franchise, owned by Nintendo and created by Japanese video game designer Shigeru Miyamoto. Serving as the company's mascot and the eponymous protagonist of the series, Mario has appeared in over 200 video games since his creation. Depicted as a short, pudgy, Italian plumber who resides in the Mushroom Kingdom, his adventures generally center upon rescuing Princess Peach from the Koopa villain Bowser. (Credit to Wikipedia)

The Timeline of Mario

1. 1981 - Donkey Kong

This is Mario in his first debut game of Donkey Kong and he was known as the jumpman.

2. 1985 - Super Mario Bros.

First official Mario game and one of the most popular games of its time.

3. 1989 - Super Mario Bros. 2

Second official Mario game and more emphasis of Luigi.

4. 1990-1992 - Super Mario World

Super Mario World is often considered one of the greatest video games of all time. It sold over 20 million copies worldwide and introduced Yoshi.

5. 1997 - Super Mario 64

Super Mario 64 was one of the first 3D games in the Mario series and of its times.

6. 2002 - Super Mario Sunshine

Super Mario Sunshine was the first game to introduce Mario in his 3D rendering that is used till today.

7. 2006-Present - New Super Mario Bros. and beyond

New Super Mario Bros. in 2006 gave the Mario series a new life and today Mario is still known as one of the most popular game characters of all time.
